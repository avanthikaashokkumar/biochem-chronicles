About

Science explained through projects, not buzzwords.

I am building The BioChem Chronicles as a student portfolio and science communication project. Each page explains a project in plain language, then adds the technical detail needed to understand how the device or concept works.

The site is written carefully so it does not overclaim. Arduino builds are described as prototypes. Planned bioinformatics work is labeled as planned. Health-related devices include safety notes.
